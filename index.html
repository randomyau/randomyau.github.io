The Deadpool Killogy

In this famously gorey trilogy, Deadpool proceeds to eradicate the existence of comics and himself.

Schizophrenia

You may know the Deadpool is a schizophrenic, meaning he has voices speaking within his head. These are known as the white and yellow boxes, with the white one being the serious voice and yellow the funny voice.

Deadpool Kills the Marvel Universe

Deadpool of Earth-12101 is taken to a doctor to heal him from his insanity, but this doctor was revealed to be Psycho-man, who brainwashes the voices out of deadpool and replaces it with a red box. He then proceeds to kill the likes of The Fantastic Four, Uatu the Watcher, Luke Cage, Spiderman, Ms. Marvel, Ant-man, and more, effectively killing the Marvel Universe.

Deadpool Killustrated

Deadpool has had an epiphany and realizes that the reason he and all the other costumed characters are trapped in an endless cycle of killing and resurrection is that they are all fictional characters and the only way to end the cycle is to kill EVERYONE. He temporarily spares Mad Thinker, Leader, Egghead and some of the other genius super-villains to work for him. Mad Thinker explains that they have come up with a theory based on his idea that they are all fictional characters; if he travels through the Nexus of All Realities to the realm of the Classics and murders those characters, comic heroes and villains will have nothing to be based upon and thus disappear. Deadpool enters the Nexus after leaving HERBIEs of Death to execute the super-villains and murders Don Quixote, Moby Dick, Sherlock Holmes, Ebenezer Scrooge, and more, eliminating the inspiration of his fellow costumed heroes.

Deadpool Kills Deadpool

Cut to Earth-616 where Deadpool is battling Ultimatum's giant robot. He's feeling a little out-classed but it's the Deadpool Corps to the rescue bearing a grim warning. Leather-clad Deadpool has found them and callously murders Dogpool and Golden Age Deadpool. Deadpool defeats him by using his own bodyslide technology against him. When Deadpool joins Lady and Kid Deadpool aboard their ship, a Watcher appears and informs him that Deadpools across the multiverse have been targeted for extinction as "Deadpool is the progenitor of all things." This is getting long. All the deadpools die. All of them.
